OUR SERVICES

At our classy household decor business, we offer a wide range of services to help our customers achieve their dream home decor. Here are ten of the services we provide and an explanation of each:

  1. Interior Design Consultation: Our interior design consultation service helps our clients to understand their interior design needs and style preferences. Our experienced designers work with them to create a unique and personalized home decor plan.

  2. Custom Furniture: We offer custom furniture services to create unique pieces that fit perfectly into any space. Our expert craftsmen use the highest quality materials to create one-of-a-kind pieces that reflect the client's personal style.


  3. Upholstery: Our upholstery service helps to revive old furniture by giving it a new look and feel. We use high-quality fabrics to create custom upholstery that matches the client's style and taste.


  4. Window Treatments: We provide a wide range of window treatment options including blinds, shades, and curtains. Our expert designers help to choose the right style, color, and material to complement the client's home decor.

  5. Wall Coverings: Our wall covering services include wallpaper, paint, and custom murals. Our designers work with the client to choose the right style and color for their space.

  6. Lighting: Our lighting services include custom lighting design and installation. We help our clients to create the perfect ambiance by selecting the right lighting fixtures for their space.


  7. Flooring: We offer a wide range of flooring options including hardwood, tile, and carpet. Our designers work with the client to choose the right material and style for their space.


  8. Accessories: Our accessories service includes the selection and placement of decorative items such as art, rugs, and vases. Our designers help to create a cohesive look by choosing items that complement the overall style of the home.

  9. Space Planning: Our space planning service helps our clients to maximize their space by creating functional layouts that work for their lifestyle. Our designers work with the client to create a plan that meets their specific needs.

  10. Project Management: Our project management service ensures that each project is completed on time and within budget. We coordinate with contractors and vendors to ensure a smooth and stress-free experience for our clients.

In summary, our household decor business offers a comprehensive range of services to help our clients achieve their dream home decor
